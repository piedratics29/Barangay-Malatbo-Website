Local History & Vision

Barangay Malatbo

Barangay Malatbo is tucked beautifully into the lush rolling landscapes of **Ginatilan, Cebu, Philippines**. Legend has it that the area was named after the majestic "Latbo" wild creepers and thick vines that nested in the fertile heights of the mountains, which original ancestors relied upon for building materials and traditional healing.

Historically representing a vital mountain settlement, Malatbo has emerged from a simple agricultural community of copra growers into a progressive local government unit. Today, we stand as a beacon of high-tech civic modernization, ecological tourism, and absolute fiscal transparency.

visibility The Vision

To realize a modern, ecologically rich smart community, driven by progressive policies, absolute fiscal trust, and dynamic, climate-ready agricultural infrastructure.

explore The Mission

To serve residents via automated civic systems, build robust farm-to-market trails, offer exemplary diagnostics, and enrich tourist locations while preserving cultural roots.

workspace_premium Our Core Civic Values

Integrity & Transparency (Dungog)

No secrets. Every municipal budget audit, street light invoice and project milestone is fully cataloged online for residential inspection.

Future-Ready Resilience (Kakusgan)

Adapting modern green-grids, telemedicine pipelines, and digital interfaces to assure absolute community support during natural disasters.

Bayanihan Spirit (Ampo)

Unifying our 7 agricultural Puroks into a close-knit collective sharing materials, technology, welfare services, and water management.
